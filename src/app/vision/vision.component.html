THIS IS LIFEBANK

We believe no African should die because of the shortage of medical supplies.

LifeBank is committed to rescuing women, children and other vulnerable people through ensuring access to blood, oxygen, vaccines and other critical supplies.

Multi-Modal Delivery Network

We deliver using bikes, boats, drones, contactless pick up and delivery maruwa and trucks.

We bring flexibility and cost savings to our distribution network to deliver faster, safer and cheaper to every patient all the time.

Our Tech

We are building technology to improve blood safety, access and affordability of essential supplies. We combine accessible technology with deep tech to build tools that ensure universal access.
