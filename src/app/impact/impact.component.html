LifeBank is in the business of saving lives. Impact is in our DNA and it is what keeps us going. We are deeply committed to helping the most vulnerable members of our community.

01

Lives Saved

Since we launched in 2016, we have rescued over 9,000 people and counting. Everyday when we go to work, our job is saving the lives of women in childbirth, children with malaria and or pneumonia, and people with chronic and emergency conditions. Our goal is to grow the number of lives we’ve touched exponentially and across Africa.

02

Communities Helped

We believe in the universal access to healthcare. We believe healthcare is a human right and poverty should not be a barrier to healthcare access. Thus, we work to ensure all communities, no matter how poor, have access to critical supplies. Our BOAT by LifeBank platform provides deeply subsidized, or absolutely free access to blood and oxygen across 5 states in Nigeria, and Kenya. BOAT by LifeBank is a trust and a foundation that funds the procurement of blood and oxygen to poor and vulnerable Nigerians and Kenyans. We work with doctors to identify low-income patients in need. The foundation has helped 423 patients in the first season. Season 2’s goal is to raise 10 million naira for 3,500 patients. Join Us.

03

Jobs Created

Poverty is a human rights issue and jobs are a path to reducing poverty. We work with local communities to provide training and experience to vulnerable citizens thereby improving their livelihoods. We improve prosperity for our communities by creating expertise in our blood riders, moving them from Okada (bike) guys, to Expert Medical Dispatch Riders with better pay and job satisfaction.

04

We are working towards the realization of 4 key SDGs. 1,3,8,9.

We strive to reduce the poverty rate due to the unexpected high cost of healthcare by covering for the cost of blood, oxygen and medicine for low-income Nigerians with BOAT By LifeBank (Blood, Oxygen Access Trust).

Our core services - discovery and delivery of critical supplies ensures that everyone has access to good health and improved well being.

We believe in higher levels of productivity and technological innovation. We are a tool in the provision of full and productive employment, and decent work, for all.

At LifeBank, we strive to provide sustainable innovative solutions to problems in Africa's healthcare industry using technology.
