Our Innovation

We are constantly innovating around the medical supply chain

01

SmartBag

SmartBag is a blockchain-powered product that helps patients and health providers discover the safety records of blood and blood products. Information about the processes involved in blood supply is recorded on a blockchain, preserving its integrity. When scanned, the SmartBag gives hospitals access to all the details about the donation, collection, screening, storage, and delivery procedures involved in the blood and blood components transfused into their patients.

An innovation that uses inventory management technology combined with artificial intelligence to prevent medical supplies stock-outs at hospitals.

BOAT By LifeBank

Blood and Oxygen Access Trust is a fund created to supply critical supplies like blood, oxygen and medications for the treatment of low-income patients who need it but cannot afford it.
